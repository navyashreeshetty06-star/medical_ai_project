1. Who We Are

2. Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

  • To use AI to save lives.
We aim to support hospitals and clinics by providing fast AI-based analysis, especially in places where trained radiologists are limited.

3. Why We Built This System

Today, Pneumonia remains one of the leading causes of child mortality. Many hospitals do not have radiologists available 24/7, and manual diagnosis can take time. With AI, the analysis becomes:

  • Instant
  • Consistent
  • Highly accurate
  • Always available

This system is designed so that medical professionals can upload an X-ray image and get an immediate prediction.

4. What Our System Does

Our AI model analyzes chest X-ray images and classifies them into:


  • NORMAL
  • PNEUMONIA

The system quickly reads the uploaded image, processes it using deep learning, and predicts the condition with confidence score.
